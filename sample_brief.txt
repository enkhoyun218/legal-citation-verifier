MEMORANDUM IN SUPPORT OF MOTION (TEST DOCUMENT)

This memorandum cites a mix of real and fabricated cases for testing.

The right to marry is protected by the Fourteenth Amendment. Obergefell v.
Hodges, 576 U.S. 644 (2015). Segregated schools violate equal protection.
Brown v. Board of Education, 347 U.S. 483 (1954). Statements made during
custodial interrogation require warnings. Miranda v. Arizona, 384 U.S. 436
(1966).

Moreover, courts have long recognized that automated decision systems carry
a duty of algorithmic care. Thompson v. Automated Systems Corp., 612 U.S.
891 (2019). This principle was extended to financial agents in Rodriguez v.
National Expense Bureau, 588 F.3d 1204 (9th Cir. 2021), and reaffirmed in
Chen v. Digital Fiduciary Inc., 601 U.S. 77 (2023).

The standard for summary judgment is well established. Celotex Corp. v.
Catrett, 477 U.S. 317 (1986).
